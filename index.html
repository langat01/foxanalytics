About Fox Analytics

Your trusted partner in business intelligence and data solutions

Who We Are

Fox Analytics is a premier provider of business intelligence, data analytics, and inventory management solutions with operations across East Africa. Founded in Nairobi, Kenya, we specialize in transforming complex data into clear, actionable strategies that drive business growth.

Our team of experts combines cutting-edge technology with deep industry knowledge to deliver customized solutions for businesses of all sizes - from small retailers to large enterprises.

Our Mission

To empower East African businesses with data-driven insights that optimize operations, increase profitability, and foster sustainable growth through innovative analytics solutions.

Our Vision

To become the leading provider of business intelligence solutions in East Africa, recognized for transforming how businesses leverage data for competitive advantage.
